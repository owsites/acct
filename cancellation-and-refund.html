Cancellation and Refund Policy

Effective Date: 01 September 2024

At the Association of Critical Care Technologists (ACCT), we take great care to ensure transparency and fairness in our services, including membership applications. Please carefully review our policy below:

1. Membership Application Cancellation

Once a membership application has been submitted and approved by ACCT, it cannot be cancelled. Memberships are processed immediately after submission, and all members gain access to our exclusive resources and benefits. Therefore, cancellation requests for memberships will not be entertained.

2. Non-Refundable Payments

All payments made towards membership fees are non-refundable. This applies to both Life Memberships and Student Memberships. By submitting your membership application, you agree to these terms and conditions and understand that no refund will be issued under any circumstances.

3. Payment Terms

All payments are processed securely through our payment partners. Once your payment is confirmed, you will receive a confirmation email from ACCT. Please retain this email for future reference.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this policy, please feel free to reach out to us at:
