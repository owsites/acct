Education in Critical Care Technology

As a leading organization in the field of Critical Care Technology, the Association of Critical Care Technologists (ACCT) is committed to providing students and professionals with the resources they need to advance their education. Critical Care Technology is a specialized area of healthcare that focuses on the use of advanced technology in managing critically ill patients, primarily in the ICU and emergency settings.

Major Courses in Critical Care Technology in India
  • Diploma in Critical Care Technology (DCT) - 2 years
  • B.Sc. in Critical Care Technology - 3 years
  • M.Sc. in Critical Care Technology - 2 years
  • Postgraduate Diploma in Critical Care Technology - 1 year
