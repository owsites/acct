Shipping and Delivery Policy

Effective Date: 01 September 2024

At the Association of Critical Care Technologists (ACCT), we are committed to ensuring that your membership materials are delivered to you in a timely manner. Please review our shipping and delivery policy below.

1. Issuance of Membership ID Cards and Certificates

Upon successful approval of your membership application, ACCT will issue an official Membership ID Card and Membership Certificate. These materials are provided to all members as part of the membership package.

2. Delivery Method

ACCT ships Membership ID Cards and Certificates via India Post’s Speed Post service at no additional cost to the member. There are no hidden fees or shipping charges for this service.

3. Estimated Processing Time

The issuance of Membership ID Cards and Certificates may take anywhere from 2 weeks to 1 month after your membership is approved. Processing times may vary based on the volume of applications or other factors beyond our control.

4. Shipping Duration

Once shipped, the delivery of your Membership ID Card and Certificate is dependent on India Post’s Speed Post service. Delivery times vary depending on your location within India and the standard practices of India Post.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding the shipping and delivery of your membership materials, please contact us at:
