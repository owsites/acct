Benefits

National Registry - Accreditation

Every registered member is provided an authenticated membership certificate and ID Card that can be verified by employers online.

ACCT Conference

Be a part of India's one and only national convention for critical care technology with CCTs/ICTs from all over the country.

Unlimited Access to ACCT Journal

Get free full editions of the 'Journal of Critical Care Medicine & Technology' (JCCMT) and have your clinical papers published without any processing charges.

Job Board

Exclusive access to job opportunities in multi-specialty hospitals for member CCTs/ICTs (India and Abroad).

CME & Workshops

Members get special free access and 50% discounts on online and offline certifications.

Membership Categories

Registered Member (Life Membership)

Graduation in Diploma/BSc./MSc. in Critical Care/Intensive Care Technology. Only Life Members are allowed to have national registry accreditation and may be nominated for election.

Student Member

Subsidized membership fee. Get access to ACCT Journal and Free CME/Workshops. Automatically get converted to 'Life Membership' without additional fees when you submit your graduation/degree certificate.

Fellowship

Fellow of the Association of Critical Care Technologists (FACCT) is a prestigious, honorary title available to professionals who have made significant contributions to the field of critical care technology through education, research or advocacy.
Fellowship is granted only through a majority vote.

Associate Member

International critical care/intensive care technologists or scientists / Other Allied Health professionals are allowed this membership pathway. Get free full access to the ACCT Journal and CME activities.Please contact us via email for associate membership requests.
