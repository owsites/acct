Services Offered by ACCT

As the premier organization for Critical Care Technologists in India, the Association of Critical Care Technologists (ACCT) provides a wide range of services to its members and the wider healthcare community. These services are designed to promote professional growth, ensure global standards of practice, and offer recognition to critical care technologists who work tirelessly to save lives in ICUs across the country.

Membership ID Card and Certificate

Upon joining the ACCT, members are issued an official Membership ID Card and Certificate of affiliation. These credentials signify the member’s commitment to upholding the standards and ethics set forth by the association. Both the ID card and certificate provide instant recognition of the member’s professional status, allowing them access to exclusive resources, training programs, and events hosted by ACCT.



Good Standing Certificate

The Good Standing Certificate is an essential document for any Critical Care Technologist who wishes to apply for positions or further education both in India and abroad. This certificate is issued by ACCT to members in good standing, verifying their active participation, compliance with professional standards, and contributions to the field.

Why You Need a Good Standing Certificate?

  • Required by many international employers or educational institutions for career opportunities and higher studies.
  • Confirms that the member has adhered to the professional and ethical standards of critical care technology.
  • Validates the member’s clean professional record, making it an essential document for career mobility.
  • Recognition of skills and qualifications in countries like the UK, USA, and the Middle East.



Continuing Professional Development (CPD)

To maintain a high standard of critical care practices in India, ACCT provides various Continuing Professional Development (CPD) opportunities to its members. These opportunities include workshops, webinars, and hands-on training sessions in cutting-edge critical care technology, patient management systems, and ICU protocols.

CPD Programs Include:

  • Certification courses in advanced critical care management.
  • Updates on the latest innovations in life-support systems and monitoring technologies.
  • Hands-on sessions with advanced ICU equipment and life-support technologies.
  • Guest lectures by international experts in critical care.
