Who We Are

Welcome to ACCT.

ACCT is the first and only national organization in India dedicated to supporting and representing critical care/intensive care technologists.

Our members are highly trained professionals who play a crucial role in the management of critically ill patients, working alongside doctors and nurses to ensure optimal patient outcomes.

Why Study Critical Care Tech?

Impactful Career

Critical care technologists play a vital role in saving lives and improving patient outcomes in ICUs/ERs, providing immense job satisfaction and purpose.

Critical Care / Intensive care Technology

Cutting-Edge Knowledge

This career offers the opportunity to work with advanced medical technologies (Mechanical Ventilators, Airway devices, ECMO) and stay at the forefront of healthcare innovations.

Dip., BSc. & MSc. available

Scope for Future

With the growth of critical care and ICU infrastructure in India, critical care technologists will be in high demand, offering strong job security and competitive salaries.

Accreditation & Registration

Become a Licensed Critical Care Technologist

We have launched the national registry for critical care/intensive care technologists in India. Every registered member is provided an authenticated membership certificate and ID Card. We also certify and provide Good standing certificates for your job applications in India and abroad with online verification service.
