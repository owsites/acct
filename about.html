About ACCT India

Association of Critical Care Technologists

The Association of Critical Care Technologists (ACCT) is India's leading professional body, dedicated to the advancement, recognition, and support of Critical Care Technologists (CCTs) nationwide. As a pivotal organization within the healthcare system, we are committed to the enhancement of critical care technology and the upskilling of professionals who serve as vital contributors in ICU, emergency, and intensive care units across the country.

Who We Are

Established by healthcare pioneers, ACCT serves as the unified voice for Critical Care Technologists, advocating for improved education, training, and certification standards across India. We are the official body representing thousands of certified technologists working alongside physicians, surgeons, and nursing staff to ensure life-saving interventions for critically ill patients. Our Mission

  • • Promote excellence in critical care technology and healthcare innovation.
  • • Provide continuous education and professional development for critical care technologists.
  • • Ensure a standardized curriculum and certification process that meets global healthcare standards.
  • • Advocate for policy changes to further recognize the indispensable role of critical care technologists in modern healthcare systems.

What We Do

  • • National Registry Accreditation: ACCT offers national-level accreditation to certified professionals, which grants them eligibility for both national and international employment opportunities.
  • • Education & Certification: Through partnerships with academic institutions, ACCT develops standardized education programs, workshops, and certification exams to ensure that our members are well-equipped to face the evolving challenges of critical care.
  • • Workshops & Seminars: Regular webinars, workshops, and conferences provide continuous learning opportunities, allowing members to stay updated with cutting-edge advancements in critical care technology and patient management.
  • • Advocacy & Representation: We work closely with government health bodies and international healthcare associations to advocate for improved working conditions, better career pathways, and increased recognition of critical care technologists in India.

International Collaboration

ACCT aims to maintain strong relationships with international bodies like the Society of Critical Care Medicine (SCCM) and European Society of Intensive Care Medicine (ESICM) and national organizations like the Indian Society for Critical Care Medicine (ISCCM) to bring the best practices and innovations to Indian critical care technologists. Our partnership with prominent organizations ensures that Indian professionals stay at the forefront of healthcare technology and patient care strategies.

Why Join Us?

    Becoming a member of ACCT gives you access to:
  • Exclusive Resources: Free access to our journals, publications, and case studies from global experts in critical care.
  • Career Advancement: Networking opportunities with industry leaders, hospital administrators, and policymakers to fast-track your career.
  • Specialized Training: Hands-on workshops, certification courses, and professional development programs tailored for both students and seasoned professionals.
  • Global Recognition: Membership in ACCT connects you to a network of critical care professionals not just within India, but worldwide.
